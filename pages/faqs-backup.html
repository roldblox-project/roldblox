What is ROLDBLOX?

ROLDBLOX is a fan-made project by NotRoblox that tries to restore the classic Roblox look and UI so players can relive the platform's older aesthetic. Remember, this is not an official Roblox product. It's a community-driven effort to bring back the nostalgia of Roblox's earlier days. Players can expect a blend of classic elements and modern enhancements.

What is ROHTML?

ROHTML is a mod that accurately brings back the old 2016 ROBLOX UI to Roblox using an overlay made with HTML, CSS, JS and AutoHotKey. This makes it less invasive and easier to maintain than binary patches.

What is ROVAMPED?

ROVAMPED is a mod that changes Roblox to have a more old aesthetic to it by changing its modern icons to older icons, which can make Roblox look better in some ways. May be broken due to Roblox's new fastflag allowlist.

What are mods?

Mods are modifications made to a game or software to change its appearance or functionality. In the context of Roblox, mods can include custom UI elements, overlays, and other enhancements that aim to improve the user experience or bring back nostalgic features. And if you wonder, mods are completely safe to use as they only affect your local game client and do not modify the game server or your account in any way.

No. ROLDBLOX is a community project created by NotRoblox to bring back the old Roblox feel. We're fans, not Roblox. This project is not made, approved, or sponsored by Roblox Corporation. We use a community project trying to recreate the classic feel.

Are some ROLDBLOX mods deprecated or broken?

Yes - a few older mods are marked as broken or deprecated, usually because of things like DataModelPatch expiration or changes to Roblox that make them stop working. These versions are still kept for archival purposes, so you can look back at them even if they are no longer fully usable.

Examples include the ROLDBLOX 2016 mod, the ROLDBLOX 2006 mod, and possibly ROVAMPED in the future.

On the other hand, ROHTML will not break because it works as an overlay. It does not rely on Roblox's assets at all, which makes it much more stable over time.

What does 'DataModelPatch (DMP) expiration' mean?

'DataModelPatch expiration' is what happens when Roblox updates the platform in a way that breaks older patches. These are models that adjust or replace parts of the Roblox interface.

When a patch expires, it stops working until someone rewrites it to match the new platform changes. This is why some older mods may suddenly stop working or be marked as broken or deprecated.

In short - DMP expiration is not caused by ROLDBLOX itself, but by Roblox updating and changing things in the background. Until the patch is updated, the mod that uses it may no longer function.
