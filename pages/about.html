What is ROLDBLOX?

ROLDBLOX is a community-made project dedicated to recreating the classic 2016 Roblox interface through various mods. It aims to bring back the nostalgia of the Roblox experience from the golden days. The project includes several mods, each offering a unique take on the classic interface:

  • ROLDBLOX: A mod that recreates the 2016 Roblox interface by using the old V1 Menu, and replacing the TopBar icons with older ones.
  • ROVAMPED: A mod that gives Roblox a Modern-Retro look, combining elements of the modern interface with old-school design.
  • ROHTML: An upcoming mod that overlays an HTML recreation of the 2016 interface on top of the current Roblox App using AutoHotkey, HTML and CSS. This mod is still in development and aims to provide a seamless experience for users who want to relive the classic Roblox look.

    • These mods aim to bring back the nostalgia, customization, and community spirit of Roblox's golden era through fan-made content.
