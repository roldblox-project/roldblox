What is ROLDBLOX?

ROLDBLOX is a community-driven project dedicated to recreating the authentic, classic Roblox experience from 2016. We bring back the era you love through meticulously crafted mods and interface recreations.

ROLDBLOX

The core mod that recreates the 2016 Roblox interface using the original V1 Menu and restored TopBar icons. Perfect for the purist seeking the original feel.

ROVAMPED

A unique "Modern-Retro" fusion. ROVAMPED combines the stability of the modern platform with classic icons and design elements for a refreshed nostalgic look.

ROHTML

Our most ambitious project. An upcoming HTML/CSS overlay that perfectly recreates the 2016 website interface on top of the modern Roblox application.

Our mission is to preserve the nostalgia, customization, and community spirit of Roblox's golden era through fan-made content.
