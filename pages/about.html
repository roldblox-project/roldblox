What is ROLDBLOX?

ROLDBLOX is a community-driven initiative developed by NotRoblox, dedicated to restoring the classic Roblox experience. Our projects include:

  • ROLDBLOX: A mod that emulates the 2016 Roblox interface.
  • ROVAMPED: A modern-retro hybrid design for a refreshed nostalgic feel.
  • ROHTML: An upcoming mod that overlays the authentic 2016 interface.

    • These mods aim to bring back the nostalgia, customization, and community spirit of Roblox's golden era through fan-made content.
