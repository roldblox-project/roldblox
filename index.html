Project Discontinuation

I am deeply sorry to announce that I am discontinuing this project (at least for the time being). This decision stems from personal reasons and the constant, disruptive updates from Roblox that have made this project increasingly difficult to maintain and develop.

However, we are pleased to share that ROVAMPED will remain active! It is currently being maintained by two dedicated fans who are working hard to keep it updated and compatible with the latest versions of Roblox. A huge shout-out to them for their support!

Regarding ROHTML, development is still technically ongoing, but please do not expect a release in the near future. As mentioned, the combination of personal commitments and platform updates means I simply do not have the time to dedicate to it right now.

You are welcome to continue exploring the website, but please keep in mind that much of the information provided here is likely outdated.
